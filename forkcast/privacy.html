ForkCast iOS App

Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 30, 2026

1. Information We Collect

ForkCast collects the following information when you use the app: email address (for account creation), food diary entries and nutrition logs, recipes you save or generate, meal plans, and app usage data.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to provide and improve the ForkCast app, sync your data across devices via Apple CloudKit, and authenticate your account via Firebase.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using Google Firebase and Apple CloudKit. Food diary entries, recipes, and meal plans are stored on your device and are optionally synced via CloudKit if you enable ForkCast Pro.

4. Third-Party Services

ForkCast uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Firebase (authentication and data storage)
  • Apple CloudKit (data sync)
  • Anthropic Claude API (AI recipe generation)

These services have their own privacy policies.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

6. Data Deletion

You can delete your account and all associated data at any time from Settings → Delete Account within the app.

7. Children's Privacy

ForkCast is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

8. Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact us at: hello@forkcastrecipes.com

9. Changes

We may update this policy periodically. Continued use of the app constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
