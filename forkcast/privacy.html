ForkCast iOS App

Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 30, 2026

Overview

ForkCast is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use the ForkCast app. By using ForkCast, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

Data We Collect

ForkCast collects only the minimum data necessary to provide its features:

  • Recipes and meal plans you create or save — stored locally on your device and synced via iCloud to your other Apple devices.
  • Pantry items, shopping lists, and food diary entries — stored locally and synced via iCloud.
  • Camera and photo library access — used only when you choose to scan a recipe or food item. Images are processed on-device or sent to our AI service for recipe generation. We do not store your photos.
  • Microphone access — used only for voice control in Cook Mode. Voice data is processed locally and never transmitted or stored.
  • Speech recognition — used for hands-free cooking commands. Audio is processed on-device.
  • AI-generated content — when you use AI features, your prompts are sent to our Firebase proxy and processed by an AI service. We do not store these prompts or associate them with your identity.

iCloud and Data Sync

ForkCast uses Apple iCloud to sync your recipes, pantry, meal plans, and food diary across your Apple devices. iCloud data is governed by Apple's Privacy Policy at apple.com/privacy. We do not have access to your iCloud data.

Third-Party Services

ForkCast uses the following third-party services:

  • Firebase by Google — used to host our AI proxy functions. Firebase may collect anonymized usage and diagnostic data.
  • EDAMAM Nutrition API — used to look up nutritional information. Queries include ingredient names only, with no personally identifiable information.
  • Apple CloudKit — used for iCloud sync as described above.

We do not use advertising networks, sell your data to third parties, or share your personal information beyond what is described above.

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect your name, email address, or any account information.
  • We do not track you across apps or websites.
  • We do not use advertising identifiers.
  • We do not sell, rent, or trade your data.
  • We do not collect precise or coarse location data.

Children's Privacy

ForkCast is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

Data Retention and Deletion

All your recipes, pantry items, meal plans, and food diary entries are stored in your iCloud account and on your device. You can delete any data at any time from within the app. To delete all your data, delete the app from your device and clear your ForkCast iCloud data in Settings → your name → iCloud → Manage Storage.

Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information. All data synced via iCloud is encrypted by Apple in transit and at rest. AI requests are sent over HTTPS to our Firebase proxy.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the date at the top of this policy. Continued use of ForkCast after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns, contact us at: hello@forkcastrecipes.com
