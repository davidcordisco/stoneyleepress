David Cordisco is the founder of Stoney Lee Press LLC, an independent publishing and software studio based in Ridgefield, Connecticut. With over 15 years of experience across Apple, Lumen Technologies, and Frontier Communications in sales operations and revenue leadership, he applies that same precision and systems thinking to the products he builds independently.

Stoney Lee Press publishes software and literature with a shared philosophy: tools should be honest about what they do, and stories should earn their endings. ApplyNex and ForkCast are both live products with active users. RantLogic is in development. The Other Side of Me is his debut psychological thriller, available across all major retail platforms.

Cordisco serves as Board Treasurer for Meals on Wheels Ridgefield and on a local Board of Education. He is completing a Master of Public Administration with a concentration in Business Intelligence and Data Analytics.
