Rant to Logic iOS App

Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 24, 2026

Introduction

Rant to Logic is developed and published by Stoney Lee Press LLC ("we," "us," or "our"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the app. By using Rant to Logic, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account information: your email address and password, used to create and authenticate your account.
  • Rant content: the text you type or speak into the app, which is processed to generate Apple Shortcuts and personal automations.
  • Voice input: if you choose to speak your rant instead of typing it, your device records short audio that is transcribed to text. See "Voice Input" below for details.
  • App-stored content: the rants and Logic you create are saved to your device locally so you can re-install, re-run, or reference them later.

We do not collect location data, contacts, health data, payment information, or any other personal data beyond what is listed above. Apple handles the payment details for your in-app purchase — we never see your credit card information.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Authenticate your account and maintain your session.
  • Process your rants and generate Apple Shortcuts and personal automations.
  • Respond to support requests sent to stoneyleepress@gmail.com.
  • Improve the app experience based on how the app is used.

We do not sell your data to anyone, ever. We do not share your data with advertisers. We do not use your rants to train AI models.

Voice Input

When you use voice entry, your device records audio and converts it to text using Apple's Speech Recognition framework. Depending on your iPhone model and iOS settings, this speech recognition may happen entirely on your device, or Apple may process the audio on its servers. Apple's handling of voice data is governed by Apple's Privacy Policy at apple.com/privacy. Once the audio is transcribed, we treat the resulting text as rant content per this policy. We do not store your raw audio.

Third-Party Services

Rant to Logic integrates with the following third-party services. Your use of the app is subject to their respective privacy policies:

  • Anthropic — rant content is sent to Anthropic's Claude API to generate your shortcut or automation. Anthropic may retain submitted content for up to 30 days for trust and safety review, after which it is deleted. Anthropic does not use your rants to train its models under the default API terms. See Anthropic's Privacy Policy at anthropic.com/legal/privacy.
  • Cloudflare — we route shortcut signing traffic through Cloudflare for security and performance. Cloudflare may log connection metadata such as IP addresses and request timestamps. See Cloudflare's Privacy Policy at cloudflare.com/privacypolicy.
  • Apple Shortcuts — generated shortcuts and automations are delivered to Apple's Shortcuts app on your device. Once installed, they are stored by iOS and may be backed up to iCloud per your device settings. See Apple's Privacy Policy at apple.com/privacy.
  • Apple (Speech Recognition, In-App Purchase) — voice transcription and purchase processing are handled by Apple. See Apple's Privacy Policy.

Data Storage and Security

Your account credentials are stored securely on your device. Your rants and Logic are saved locally on your device — they are yours.

Rant content is transmitted to our backend server to generate your automation. We may temporarily log requests for debugging and abuse prevention. These logs are rotated and deleted on a regular schedule and are not maintained as a permanent database of user rants. Logs are accessible only to the app's developers.

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data in transit and at rest. No system is perfectly secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute protection against unauthorized access. We encourage you to use a strong, unique password for your account.

Data Retention

We retain your account information for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account through the app, all locally stored data is permanently removed from your device and your account credentials are removed from our authentication system.

Temporary backend logs of rant content are retained for a short period (generally less than 30 days) for debugging and abuse prevention, then automatically deleted.

Anthropic's retention of rant content sent through their API is governed by their own policies, generally up to 30 days for trust and safety review.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access a copy of the personal information we hold about you.
  • Correct inaccurate personal information.
  • Delete your account and all associated locally stored data at any time through the app.
  • Request deletion of any server-side account records.
  • Withdraw consent for data processing (which will require you to stop using the app).

To exercise any of these rights, email stoneyleepress@gmail.com. We will respond within 30 days.

If you are a resident of the European Union, the United Kingdom, or California, you may have additional rights under applicable privacy law (GDPR, UK GDPR, CCPA). Contact us at the email above to exercise them.

Children's Privacy

Rant to Logic is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately and we will delete it.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the date at the top of this policy. Continued use of Rant to Logic after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: stoneyleepress@gmail.com
